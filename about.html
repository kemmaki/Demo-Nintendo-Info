Nintendo: A Brief Overview


Nintendo is a Japanese multinational company founded in 1889 by Fusajiro Yamauchi. Originally, it started as a playing card company producing Hanafuda cards. Over the decades, Nintendo evolved into one of the most influential and iconic video game companies in the world. It gained prominence in the 1980s with the release of the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) and has since created numerous beloved franchises that have shaped the gaming industry.
